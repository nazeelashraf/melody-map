<objective>
Redesign the SheetEditor outer structure to create a canvas-first editing surface where lyrics and cues are the visual priority. Reduce the visual weight of headers, metadata cards, and arrangement panels so they frame rather than compete with the editing area.

Purpose: The current SheetEditor wraps every section in heavy card containers (bg-card, border, shadow-sm) that fragment the editing experience. A canvas-first layout removes this fragmentation and lets musicians focus on their content.
Output: Restructured SheetEditor.tsx with minimal chrome, compact metadata/arrangement areas, and a full-width canvas container.
</objective>
